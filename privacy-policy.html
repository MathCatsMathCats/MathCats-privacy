1. Information Collection

Math Cats does not collect any personal information or data from its users. The app works completely offline and does not require any account, login, or registration.

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

Math Cats is designed for children and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not:

  • Collect any personal information from children

  • Share any information with third parties

  • Include links to external websites (except optional Support Developer link)

4. Third-Party Services

Math Cats does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline without any network connectivity required.

5. Support Developer Feature

The optional "Support Developer" button may direct you to an external website for voluntary donations. This link is clearly marked and requires explicit user action to access. We do not receive any information about your interactions with these external services.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or protect. All game information stays on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.
